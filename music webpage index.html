Biography: A star was born on the 13 of june year 1999, well this day according to my mother she said this was her most happiest day ever lived on earth, cause after she gave birth to me everything is going as planned and it was even sad at the moment cause at that time my father was not with my mother anymore, at times i even cry when saying this but thank God for life and the journey I have went so far.. Actually I attended my pre school at freetown, where it was half structured and I was happy with that because i never grew on that wealthy side so I appreciate every situation i encounter, and the good thing about it my mummy was also happy with me..At high school I went to the most outstanding secondary school in sierra leone I mean "The prince of wales school" located at kingtom freetown.
